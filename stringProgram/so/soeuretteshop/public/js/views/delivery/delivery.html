 Shipping and Delivery:

Deliveries can be made only to Lebanon. If a same-day delivery is requested, and provided we receive the order before 3PM (GMT+2), we will do our best to deliver the product in the afternoon, otherwise it will be shipped the next day. For some products, delivery on Sunday is not available. In most cases, Soeurette Shop will call the recipient to agree on the time of delivery; however if the address is absent, we may leave the parcel at the front door or leave a message in the mailbox.


For Valentine (Feb 14) and Mother's Day (Mar 21):


Orders received on the same day or during the night before will be processed on best effort basis and might be delivered the next day, so please order at least 1 day ahead. Also for orders on these 2 occasions we cannot commit to specific delivery times as we will be dispatching throughout the day but we will always call your recipient to coordinate the delivery.


Order Cancellation:


A full refund is provided for orders cancelled more than 48 hours prior to delivery, otherwise the full amount will be charged or kept as credit on a case-by-case basis.


Product Return Procedures:


For non perishable items, any returns must be subject to a formal agreement between Soeurette Shop and the buyer. For any returns accepted by us, in the case of a visible defect or the non-conformity of the products delivered, we will allow the buyer to obtain the free replacement or the restitution of a credit note to his benefit, exclusive of any other indemnity or damages.
